How to use this dashboard

A quick guide for staff and admin. This page updates automatically as we add features.

1. Two modes — viewer and admin

Everyone on the team signs in with a staff passcode to view. Only the admin (Sister Pauline) can add, edit, and delete — she signs in separately using the admin passcode.

  • Staff sign-in lets you see events, tasks, and people.
  • Admin sign-in shows + Add, Edit, and Delete buttons, and unlocks the Budget tab (finances are admin-only).
  • Click Sign out in the top-right — this locks both admin and the staff passcode on the device. The welcome screen reappears.
  • Any team member can click Lock dashboard in the footer to clear the staff passcode on their device (useful on shared phones/laptops).

2. Overview tab

The home page shows four stat cards (upcoming events, open tasks, volunteers, total spend), the next event, and a live feed of recent changes. Admins can clear the activity log with the Clear activity button.

3. Events tab

  • + New Event — name, date, location, description.
  • Each event card shows date, task progress, and buttons to open tasks, edit, or delete.
  • Deleting an event also removes its tasks and expenses (it asks first).

4. Tasks & Teams tab — the main workspace

  1. Pick an event from the dropdown.
  2. Click Add from standard list… to pick common tasks (decoration, food, media, cleanup, etc.) in one go.
  3. Or click + Add Task for a custom one.
  4. On each task card, use the Assign… dropdown to add volunteers. Team members for that category show first.
  5. A Rotation suggestion shows people who haven't served recently — click Apply to accept it.
  6. Click Auto-fill rotation to assign all empty tasks automatically (caps 3 tasks per person).
  7. In the Summary card: Copy for WhatsApp, Download Excel, or Print.
  8. Click Mark done on the day as tasks are completed.

5. People tab

  • Click Load NLC Team once to import the whole roster. Safe to click again later — duplicates are skipped.
  • + Add Person — enter name and tick the teams they belong to.
  • + Add Team — create custom teams like Prayer, Youth, Kitchen.
  • Filter chips at the top show how many people are in each team.
  • The coloured number on each person's card shows their load in the selected event (green = light, red = busy).

6. Budget tab (admin-only)

Only visible when signed in as admin. Pick an event, add expenses (item, category, planned €, actual €, paid by). The three totals at the top show planned vs actual vs remaining. Click Download Excel for a full report.

7. On your phone

The dashboard works in any mobile browser. Handy during an event for ticking tasks done, adding last-minute expenses, or checking who is doing what.
